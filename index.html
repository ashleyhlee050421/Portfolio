About Me

I am an artist with experience in painting and hands-on studio practice, with a strong interest in contributing to large-scale, collaborative projects such as mural work. Although I do not have direct experience in hand-painting murals, I am highly patient, detail-oriented, and able to work carefully with consistency and precision. I am confident in my ability to focus on specific sections of a project while maintaining overall visual cohesion. I am comfortable working in collaborative environments, including guiding and working alongside others when needed. I am also flexible with scheduling and committed to contributing reliably to the progress of the project.
